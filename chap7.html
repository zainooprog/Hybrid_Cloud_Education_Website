Chapter 7: Cloud Economics & The Future Roadmap

Cloud computing is more than just a technical shift; it is a major business strategy. In this final chapter, we look at how cloud billing works, how to save money, and where the industry is headed next.

üí∞ Cloud Pricing Models

Pay-As-You-Go

Like a utility bill. You pay for resources by the hour or second. Perfect for startups or unpredictable traffic.

Reserved Instances

Commit to 1 or 3 years for massive discounts (up to 70%). Best for stable, long-term businesses.

Spot Instances

Buying spare capacity at a very low price. Best for temporary tasks that can be interrupted.

üìâ Cost Optimization

  • Right-Sizing: Matching server size to actual demand so you never overpay for unused RAM or CPU.
  • Auto-Scaling: Automatically adding or removing servers based on user traffic.
  • Snoozing: Shutting down non-essential servers at night or on weekends to save costs.
